Organic Farming

Introduction to Organic Farming

Organic farming is a method of farming system which primarily aims at cultivating the land and raising crops in such a way that the soil is kept alive and in good health.

Benefits of Organic Farming

  • No synthetic pesticides or fertilizers
  • Promotes biodiversity
  • Improves soil fertility and structure
  • Reduces pollution
